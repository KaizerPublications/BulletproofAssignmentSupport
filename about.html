FAQ

  1. Can I expect the work to be completed on time?

    2. Yes. Some papers might take longer than others depending on the research and writing involved. However, we are committed to making sure that the writer completes your order in the specified deadline. All orders are delivered within the deadline agreed with a solid commitment to quality and guidelines.

  2. Why do you need my name and email

    3. The writer has to sign his name and a contract with us before sending the paper to you. Also, we will keep your information and contact details only to indicate the progress and quality of the work done by them.

  3. When should I expect to pay for the paper?

    4. After receiving a quotation for your paper. Immediately after payment, the task is assigned to an expert writer to research, write and proofread the paper.

  4. Do you hire academic writers too?

    5. Yes. We hire full-time academic writers specifically to do written assignments. Our experts are proficient in their academic discipline and have developed a high level of expertise. As such, you must be at least a degree holder in the field you wish to write.

  5. Can you write non-English papers?

    6. Unfortunately we do not. Because we only hire English writers.

  6. Is your company associated with other companies which offer similar services?

    7. No, we only employ writers on our own. We are continuously expanding our service in order to make more people happy. Our writers have full support and motivation to perform better as they always want to do the best for you. They will be provided with all requirements and guidelines necessary for writing a paper or assignment well and successfully.

  7. Is there a free revision?

    8. We offer three free revisions if our team wrote the paper.

  8. How can you offer me the lowest price?

    9. Our prices are lower because we understand student's financial status. We offer discounts and free revisions.

  9. What is your payment policy?

    10. We accept payments via Paypal, Skrill, Google Wallet, Neteller and other popular payment methods. Our most convenient method of payment is Paypal as it ensures security and is very easy to use.

  10. What about the authenticity of the writers? Is their name fake information?

    11. The services offered by us are genuine and safe. All of our writers have degrees in their field of study and work/study in several universities.
